Bandcamp User Experience

Speaking as a music buyer, here is why I think Bandcamp's user experience is great

  1. Pricing is transparent and upfront
  2. Bandcamp remembers I always buy ALAC and defaults to that
  3. You can tip as much or as little as you want
  4. The shopping card lets you add items from multiple artists and labels, in a mix of dollars, pounds and euros then just check out
  5. Periodically Bandcamp has a day when it waives fees
  6. You can redownload anything you bought previously
  7. No special downloader application is needed, just use your browser
  8. Save your credit card info or not, choice is up to you
  9. Labels and artists both use the service

Nothing is perfect

  • Bandcamp used to require there always be a digital option but that seems to have ceased
  • No Applepay support, only Paypal
